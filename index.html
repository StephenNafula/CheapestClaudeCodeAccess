Good to know

Straight answers, upfront

What am I actually paying for?

You're prepaying for API usage across supported models, plus the setup, billing management, and support that comes with it. The gap between what you pay and the raw usage credit covers that service — shown clearly on every pack.

Does the credit expire?

Unused credit is valid for 12 months from purchase. There's no auto-renewal and nothing is ever charged without you buying a new pack.

What happens when my balance hits zero?

Requests stop and you'll get a notice before you run out. Buy another pack whenever you want to keep going — there's no obligation to renew.

Which tools does the key work with?

Any tool that accepts a standard API key, including Claude Code and Claude Cowork. If you're unsure how to wire it up, support is included.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes! Refer a friend who makes their first purchase, and you'll receive KES 199 for a successful referral.
